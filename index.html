Secretarial & Compliance

We offer comprehensive secretarial services including company registrations for Private Limited and Section 8 companies, GST, MSME, E-Anudhan, PF & ESIC registrations, annual company and GST compliance, as well as auditor appointment and audit assistance.

GEM Consultant

We provide complete support for Government e-Marketplace (GeM Portal), including tender information, bidding/BOQ, and L1 comparison. We assist with product/service catalogue uploads and OEM authorization for Q2 products.
